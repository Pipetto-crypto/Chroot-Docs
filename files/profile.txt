Follow this to install box86 and box64:

https://github.com/fengxue-jrql/box86-and-box64-for-arm64/blob/main/How%20to%20set%20the%20environment

Add these additional lines to your /etc/profile:

export DISPLAY=:0
export PULSE_SERVER=127.0.0.1
export XDG_RUNTIME_DIR=/tmp/runtime-android

Change android with your username if it's not the same
